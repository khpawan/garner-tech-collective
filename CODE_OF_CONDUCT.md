# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

The Garner Tech Collective is committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

### Positive behavior includes:

* **Being Open and Welcoming** - Actively including newcomers and helping them feel part of the community
* **Showing, Not Just Telling** - Sharing knowledge through demonstrations, code, and hands-on learning
* **Focusing on Community First** - Prioritizing collective learning over individual promotion
* **Creating Local Impact** - Contributing to projects that benefit the Garner community
* **Teaching Critical Thinking** - Helping others learn to frame problems, not just find quick answers
* **Respecting Different Perspectives** - Acknowledging that everyone has something valuable to contribute
* **Being Curious** - Asking questions and admitting when we don't know something

### Unacceptable behavior includes:

* **Recruiting or Sales Pitches** - Using the community primarily for hiring or selling products/services
* **Harassment** - Including offensive comments, personal attacks, or unwelcome attention
* **Discrimination** - Exclusion based on any personal characteristic
* **Disrespecting Privacy** - Publishing others' private information without permission
* **Monopolizing Discussions** - Preventing others from participating or sharing
* **Tech Elitism** - Making others feel inferior about their skill level or technology choices
* **Bad Faith Participation** - Attending primarily to poach members for other groups or purposes

## Enforcement

### Responsibilities

Community organizers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

### Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, including:
- Meetups and events
- Online discussions
- Community projects
- Social gatherings
- Public representations of the community

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by:
- Speaking with any community organizer at an event
- Opening an issue on this GitHub repository
- Emailing: [conduct@garnertechcollective.org] (email coming soon)

All reports will be handled with discretion and confidentiality.

### Enforcement Guidelines

Community organizers will follow these guidelines in determining consequences:

1. **First Offense - Warning**
   - Private written warning providing clarity around the nature of the violation

2. **Second Offense - Temporary Suspension**
   - Temporary suspension from community events and spaces for a specified period

3. **Third Offense - Permanent Ban**
   - Permanent expulsion from all community spaces
